Quick Tips

  • Archive

    Archive coffees you're not brewing now — they keep all tuning; restore or delete anytime.

  • Water Hardness

    Set your water hardness — it nudges grind & extraction for a better cup.

  • Edit Details

    Tap the pencil icon on a card to add or fix any missing detail — origin, variety, altitude, tasting notes.

  • Roast Date

    Shows a freshness badge based on days since roasting: Still Resting, Sweet Spot, Fading.

  • Favorites

    Tap the star to favorite a coffee — it pins to the top of your list.

  • Stacking

    Coffees from the same roastery stack automatically — swipe left/right to browse the stack.
